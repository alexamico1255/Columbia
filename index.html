Columbia, South Carolina

Location

Columbia is located in the central part of South Carolina.

About

History

Columbia has a rich history dating back to its founding in 1786.

Education

Columbia is home to several prestigious universities and colleges.

Culture

The city has a vibrant arts and music scene, with many galleries and live music venues.

Things To Do

  • Visit the South Carolina State House
  • Explore the Riverbanks Zoo and Garden
  • Enjoy outdoor activities at Congaree National Park
  • Experience the vibrant nightlife on Main Street
  • Attend a concert or event at the Colonial Life Arena
